Connect to Google Sheets so your ERP data is saved in the cloud and survives browser cache clears. All reads stay instant (localStorage cache). Writes are mirrored to Sheets silently in the background.
How to get your Apps Script URL
1Open your Google Sheet → Extensions → Apps Script 2Delete any existing code and paste the contents of ManuCost_AppsScript.js (download below) 3Click Save (floppy disk), then Deploy → New Deployment 4Type: Web App · Execute as: Me · Who has access: Anyone 5Click Deploy → copy the Web App URL (starts with https://script.google.com/macros/s/…) 6Paste that URL in the field below and click Connect
This URL is saved in your browser and never leaves your device.
Push to Sheets

Upload all current data from this browser to Google Sheets. Use after first connecting, or after working offline.

Pull from Sheets

Download all data from Google Sheets into this browser. Use on a new device or after another user made changes.
